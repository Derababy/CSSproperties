The difference between Paddings, Margins and Borders:

Although they are all properties of the CSS Box Model;
Padding is the inner space between the content and the border of your box while Margin is the outer space surrounding the box that clears an area outside the border and then Border is the perimeter of the box that goes around the padding and content, it is used to set the border of an element.
